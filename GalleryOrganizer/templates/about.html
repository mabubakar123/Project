About Page

  • Gallery Organizer is an Object Detector App that allows User to Automate Categorization of their Images.
  • This App can Categorize Images into 12 Categories on the basis of 80 Objects:
    1. PEOPLE: person
    2. VEHICLES: bicycle, car, motorbike, bus, train, truck, boat, traffic light, stop sign, parking meter
    3. AEROPLANE: aeroplane
    4. ANIMALS AND BIRDS: bird, cat, dog, horse, sheep, cow, elephant, bear, zebra, giraffe
    5. SPORTS: frisbee, skis, snowboard, sports ball, kite, baseball bat, baseball glove, skateboard, surfboard, tennis racket
    6. DINING: bottle, wine glass, cup, fork, knife, spoon, bowl, microwave, oven, toaster, sink, refrigerator
    7. FOOD: banana, apple, sandwich, orange, broccoli, carrot, hot dog, pizza, donut, cake
    8. FURNITURE: chair, sofa, bed, diningtable, bench
    9. ELECTRIC APPLIANCES: tvmonitor, laptop, mouse, remote, keyboard, cell phone
    10. BAGS: backpack, handbag, suitcase
    11. HOUSEHOLD GOODS: pottedplant, clock, vase
    12. OTHERS: fire hydrant, umbrella, tie, book, scissors, teddy bear, toilet, toothbrush, hair drier and UNIDENTIFIED-THINGS
  • Gallery Organizer also allows Users to Edit Images and Make Video Clips
